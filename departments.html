Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for all heart-related conditions. From early diagnosis to advanced treatment, our team of experienced cardiologists uses state-of-the-art technology to manage a wide range of cardiac issues, including heart attacks, arrhythmias, heart failure, and hypertension. We offer personalized treatment plans, non-invasive and interventional procedures, and continuous monitoring to ensure the best outcomes for our patients. At Careplus, your heart health is our top priority.

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics Department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of conditions affecting the bones, joints, muscles, and spine. Whether it's a sports injury, fracture, arthritis, or chronic joint pain, our skilled orthopedic surgeons and physiotherapists are here to provide expert care. We offer both surgical and non-surgical treatments, including joint replacements, arthroscopic procedures, and personalized therapy programs. At Careplus, we are committed to helping you regain mobility, reduce pain, and improve your quality of life.

Pediatrics

At Careplus, we believe in partnering with parents to support every stage of a child's health journey with expert guidance and personalized attention.
